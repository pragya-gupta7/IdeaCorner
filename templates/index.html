Some of our projects

Audio surveillance using Quadcopters

Our motivation was to design something that can reduce our field work for acoustic monitoring. We thought about to use quadcopters. Then we researched about it and learnt about challenges and feasibility of this idea. We proposed our this solution to our “Noise pollution control” course instructor Prof. Prasoon K. Singh

Powering Humanity on Mars

We participated in a Martian challenge 2021.In that, we presented our ideas on Martian Energy. Our ideas were on Retractable solar plates, linking solar energy with carbon capture technology, and asteroid mining. We got special mention for the technical approach for this research article.

9 mins

Landfill-Siting based on waste generation rate

Landfill siting is required to ensure that the chosen location meets environmental regulations and standards,standards, such as soil and groundwater protection, air quality, and odor control. In this project, we have identified potential sites and chosen the final site based on our community waste generation rates.
